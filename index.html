AN AUDIENCE HYPNOTISED

"𝖂𝖍𝖊𝖗𝖊 𝕮𝖔𝖓𝖘𝖎𝖔𝖚𝖘𝖓𝖊𝖘𝖘 𝕸𝖊𝖊𝖙𝖘 𝕾𝖔𝖚𝖓𝖉"

A fusion of rhythm, texture, and transcendence—Glass Beams channels sounds from the cosmic ether, crafting sonic landscapes that dissolve the boundaries between listener and experience. Through hypnotic grooves, celestial synths, and ancient echoes, they guide audiences into a state of deep immersion, where sound becomes vision and rhythm becomes ritual.

THE VISION

Born from mystery and shrouded in mysticism, Glass Beams exists beyond genre, weaving together elements of psychedelic, world, and electronic music. Drawing inspiration from lost histories, cosmic energies, and the frequencies of the unseen, their music is an invitation to explore altered states of consciousness—whether through intimate listening or the collective energy of a live performance.

LATEST TRANSMISSIONS

A new sonic odyssey, Stellar Echoes, is set to arrive with the next full moon, inviting listeners on a journey through cosmic soundscapes and hypnotic rhythms. As the energy builds, Glass Beams announces the Ethereal Gatherings, a series of immersive live performances in Toronto and Montreal, where the collective consciousness converges to experience music as ritual.
